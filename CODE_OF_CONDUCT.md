# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free environment for everyone.

### Our Standards
- **Be respectful**: Treat everyone with kindness and professionality.
- **Be inclusive**: Welcome diverse backgrounds and perspectives.
- **Be constructive**: Focus on positive contributions and feedback.

### Enforcement
Unacceptable behavior can be reported to the project team. We will investigate all complaints and take appropriate action.
